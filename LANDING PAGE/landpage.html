ABOUT US

At Deiparine Community Hospital, we believe quality healthcare should be accessible, compassionate, and close to home. Located at 422 C. Delos Cientos Street, Poblacion 6020 Sibonga, Philippines, our hospital provides a full range of medical services—from emergency care and general medicine to pediatrics, surgery, and maternal health. With a team of dedicated doctors, nurses, and staff, we combine modern facilities with a personal touch, ensuring every patient receives safe, reliable, and affordable care. More than just a hospital, we are a partner in the community’s health and wellness.

✨ Your health, our commitment.
